LEG DAY — HOME PROTOCOL

No equipment. No excuses. One focused day to wake your legs up from their long vacation.

FOUNDATION

Deep Bodyweight Squats

Primary compound movement. Builds quad strength, glute power, and full lower-body coordination.

Form: Hips back first, knees track toes, chest up, full depth.

Breathing: Inhale down, forceful exhale up.

Common errors: Half reps, heels lifting, knees collapsing.

Prescription: 4 sets × 12–20 reps (slow control).

UNILATERAL

Reverse Lunges

Single-leg strength that exposes imbalance and improves knee safety.

Form: Step back, front heel drives, torso tall.

Breathing: Inhale stepping back, exhale up.

Common errors: Leaning forward, short steps.

Prescription: 3–4 sets × 10–14 reps per leg.

ISOMETRIC

Wall Sit Hold

Pure quad tension. No movement. Mental and muscular endurance.

Form: Thighs parallel, back flat, no hands.

Breathing: Slow and controlled.

Common errors: Standing too high, holding breath.

Prescription: 2–3 holds × 45–90 seconds.

POSTERIOR

Glute Bridges

Direct glute activation to protect knees, spine, and increase power.

Form: Drive through heels, squeeze glutes at top.

Breathing: Exhale up, inhale down.

Common errors: Lower-back arching, rushing reps.

Prescription: 3–4 sets × 15–25 reps.

LOWER LEG

Standing Calf Raises

Builds ankle strength, endurance, and explosive capacity.

Form: Full stretch bottom, hard squeeze top.

Breathing: Exhale up, inhale down.

Common errors: Bouncing, partial reps.

Prescription: 4 sets × 20–30 slow reps.

FINISHER

Jump Squats

Explosive output. Trains speed, power, and nervous system.

Form: Explode up, land soft, core tight.

Breathing: Sharp exhale on jump.

Common errors: Hard landings, sloppy reps.

Prescription: 3 sets × 8–12 quality jumps.

Split logic: Upper → Legs → Cardio → Abs → Rest

Train with intent. Recover like it matters.
