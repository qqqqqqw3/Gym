THE GODLY BODY TRAINING PROTOCOL

Forged at home. Built through discipline.

Main Upper-Body Workout

1. Decline Diamond Push-Ups

  • Targets: Chest & Triceps
  • 4 sets × 6–10 reps
  • Slow, controlled, full depth

2. Explosive Diamond Push-Ups

  • Targets: Triceps & Power
  • 3–4 sets × 5–8 reps
  • Hands leave the floor

3. Pike Push-Ups (Feet Elevated)

  • Targets: Shoulders
  • 4 sets × 8–12 reps
  • Head goes straight down

4. Plank to Push-Up

  • Targets: Shoulders, Triceps, Core
  • 3 sets × 30–45 seconds
  • No hip movement

5. Wall Push-Hold

  • Targets: Chest, Shoulders, Triceps
  • 2–3 sets × 30–60 seconds
  • Maximum effort

Finisher (Chest / Shoulders / Biceps)

Towel Biceps Curls

  • 10 reps + 10–15s isometric hold
  • 3 rounds total

Consistency beats destruction. Always.
